About Me

As a person fueled by both creativity and analytics, it made sense to pursue a life in UX/UI design. With a passion in this field, I specialize in crafting a story within the research process that shows empathy and understanding for the user of an app or website. Driven to solve stakeholder and user problems with UX, I then implement my expertise in UI to design a compelling product that is visually delightful and intuitive...one that fills a human need.

User research, user empathy, and qualitative interviews are among my UX skills. For UI, I am skilled at designing beautiful apps and websites with Adobe XD and Sketch. In general, tools such as Miro, InVision, and Trello are also applied.
